Chez Bob: The Grad Student Food Co-Op

Get your free snacks from Chez Bob!

  1. Go to CSE 3154.
    • The grad lounge, on the third floor near the stairs, is the home of Chez Bob. The door is locked, but the lounge is often occupied, so someone should be able to let you in.
  2. Log in to the computer or soda machine.
    • Scan the barcode at the top of this page.
      Both the computer and soda machine have barcode scanners.
    • or: Use the login name visitday.
  3. Buy something!
    • For soda: push the appropriate button on the soda machine.
    • For any other item: scan its barcode. You can find snacks in the refrigerator, freezer, or on the shelves.
  4. Want to know more?
    • Ask a student or anyone else in the grad lounge! Chez Bob is widely used, so most people you meet can probably tell you more about it.

We don't have limitless funds, so please limit yourself to one or two free items.

What is Chez Bob?

Chez Bob is a department food co-op run by grad students, and located in the grad student lounge (CSE 3154). We buy food and drinks in bulk from Costco, and sell them to grad students (and faculty and staff and visitorsâ€¦) at just slightly above cost.

Chez Bob runs on an honor system: food and drinks made available in the grad lounge, and people pay for them by leaving money in a box in the grad lounge. (And the best part is: the honor system actually works!)

To make things even easier, we've created the Bank of Bob (BoB) and a computer system for tracking purchases. Users can create an account, deposit money into their account, purchase items with a barcode scanner, and have the purchase price automatically deducted from their account. The BoB computer will automatically track how much each user has left to purchase food (or how much Bob is owed).

What does this mean to me?

Free snacks! We've created a Chez Bob account for you during visit days. Feel free to buy an item or two on us while you're here.

If for some reason you aren't getting enough other free food and want to buy more from Chez Bob, you can deposit money into the visitday account or even create an account of your own.

How do I log in?

To log in to the Chez Bob computer, you have a few options:

  • Type in a username at the Chez Bob computer.
  • Scan a user barcode with the barcode scanner.
For the soda machine:
  • Log in to the Chez Bob computer and select "Soda Login".
  • Log in with an on-screen keyboard at the soda machine.
  • Scan a user barcode with the barcode scanner.
  • Register a fingerprint with the system, and log in by simply scanning a finger.
  • (Under development.) Log in via a face-recognition system.

How do I buy items?

Even though we're letting you take a few items for free, we'd still like you to scan them as usual, so we can keep track.

To "purchase" items:

  • Any drinks vended by the soda machine while logged in are tracked.
  • Any other items with barcodes can be scanned at either the soda machine or Chez Bob computer.
  • For a few miscellaneous items without barcodes, use the "Extra Items" menu on the Chez Bob computer.

If you're curious about prices:

  • All items in the soda machine are $0.45, except bottled water (which is $0.25).
  • Scanning any item at the Chez Bob computer will display the price.
  • The "Extra Items" menu lists prices for these extra items.
